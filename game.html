ℹ️ What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a numerical value calculated from a person's weight and height. It provides a simple, inexpensive screening tool for weight categories.

Formula (Metric):
BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height² (m²)

Note: BMI is a general indicator and does not account for muscle mass, age, or gender differences.
